FAQ

Before you go live

Who can see my location?

Only active users on Spotlight can see your live spot, and only while you are checked in. You can turn it off anytime from the map.

How does trust score work?

Trust score changes over time from post-meet feedback and behavior signals. It is meant to reward reliability and respectful interactions.

What if someone behaves badly?

You can report users directly from profile and match flows. Reports are reviewed by admin tools and action can include blocking and suspension.

Can I match with multiple people at once?

No. Spotlight allows one active match at a time to keep plans focused and reduce spam behavior.
